How it works

From video files to finished disc in four steps.

1

Add your video files

Select one or more MKV, MP4, or M2TS files. Disc Forge automatically detects all embedded audio, subtitle, and chapter tracks using ffprobe.

2

Choose your tracks

Check or uncheck exactly which audio languages and subtitle tracks you want included. Full codec info shown for every stream.

3

Build the disc image

Click Build Disc Image. Disc Forge muxes your content into a proper Blu-ray structure and packages it as a UDF 2.5 ISO file.

4

Burn to disc

Insert a blank BD-R and click Burn to Disc. Your disc is ready in minutes, with the disc ejected automatically when done.
